# Reservation Policies

## Booking Rules
- Reservations accepted for parties of 1–10 guests
- Parties larger than 10 require special arrangements — call the restaurant
- Reservations can be made up to 30 days in advance
- Same-day reservations accepted based on availability

## Cancellation Policy
- Cancel at least 2 hours before reservation time
- No-shows may result in a waitlist for future bookings

## Seating
- Indoor and outdoor seating available (weather permitting)
- High chairs available for children
- Wheelchair accessible

## Wait Times
- Walk-ins welcome, but reservations are recommended on weekends
- Typical wait for walk-ins: 15–30 minutes on weekdays, 30–60 minutes on weekends

## Special Requests
- Dietary restrictions accommodated — please mention when booking
- Birthday/anniversary celebrations — let us know in advance
